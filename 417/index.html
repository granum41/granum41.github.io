FAQ

What does a precinct committeeman do?

A precinct committeeman is your neighborhood republican representative. We represent our neighbors views, vote on resolutions, and help recommend candidates.

How to I find my precinct?

Fill out your address on the voteidaho.gov website.

How do I register to vote?

Go to voteidaho.gov where you can: 1. Check your current registration status, this will tell you if you're registered republican and the address in which you're registered. 2. Register to Vote if you haven't already. I highly recommend doing this prior to Election day to make your experience fast and easy.
